Who Are We?

Our Story

As a software engineer working remotely with hiring agencies, I experienced frustrations caused by a lack of transparency in remote work arrangements. Issues like unclear job expectations and unpredictable payments were widespread, affecting talented professionals globally.

Determined to create a better solution, I founded KeepWorkin to prioritize transparency, trust, and clarity for businesses and remote talent. KeepWorkin streamlines hiring, enabling companies to connect with vetted professionals and skilled talent to find reliable opportunities—eliminating the challenges and inefficiencies of remote work. This vision aligns with fostering economic growth, promoting inclusive and sustainable employment, reducing inequalities, and building strong partnerships.

1

Our Vision

To empower a global workforce where skilled talent and businesses connect seamlessly and transparently, regardless of location.

We envision a world where remote work is accessible and driven by clarity, trust, and fair practices, enabling growth and innovation across borders.

By bridging global gaps, we support equitable opportunities, economic advancement, and sustainable collaborations.

2

Our Mission

To redefine the hiring process by building a platform that fosters genuine connections between businesses and pre-vetted talent. At KeepWorkin, we aim to eliminate the barriers and uncertainties of remote work by providing a transparent, user-centric experience that simplifies hiring, collaboration, and team management.

Our mission ensures companies find the right fit while professionals pursue meaningful work in an environment of trust and fairness, supporting global economic development and inclusivity.
